  • Where do i find MobiKlinic Office/Headquater in Uganda?

  • What are the products of Mobiklinic?

    We have two products, MobiLearn where you learn basic healthcare online to help help your home or community incase of any medical emergency. Then digital tool is a mobile app that is used by community Healthcare providers to access doctors in town and nearby health facilities.
